## What is this project?

Unlike a traditional sponsored campaign, this is an AI Short Drama co-creation project. Rather than creating a single advertisement, we work together with creators to develop an original AI short drama where the creator becomes the main character.

The story is tailored around the creator's personality, content style, and audience, allowing the final result to feel like a natural extension of their existing content rather than a traditional commercial.

## How the project works

A typical AI short drama consists of around 10–20 episodes.

• The first few episodes are released for free to introduce the story.
• The remaining episodes are unlocked on our platform as premium content.

## Creative Process

We don't start by writing a script.

Instead, we first get to know your content style, personality, and audience.

From there, our content team develops several story directions for you to choose from before moving into script development.

You'll have the opportunity to:

- Review the story outline
- Share ideas about the storyline, genre, or characters
- Review the final script before production begins

If there are any creative ideas you'd like to explore, we'd love to develop them together.

## Collaboration Models

### 1. Fixed Project Fee

A one-time payment for the entire AI short drama project.

### 2. Revenue Share

Creators receive 80% of the Net Revenue generated by the project.

Rather than relying solely on your own promotion, the series will also be distributed globally through our platform.

The better the project performs, the more both sides benefit.

For creators with highly engaged audiences, we often recommend the Revenue Share model because it offers stronger long-term earning potential than a traditional one-time campaign fee.
