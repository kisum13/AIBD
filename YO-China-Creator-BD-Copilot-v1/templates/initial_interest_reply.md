# Initial Interest Reply

## Use When

Use this template when:

- We only sent an initial invitation email.
- The creator replies briefly with interest, asks for a project introduction, gives a simple quote/rate, or asks no more than two simple questions.
- The creator has not listed many detailed contract/licensing/budget questions.

Do not compress this into one long paragraph. Preserve email spacing, headings, bullet points, and sample links.

## Template

Hi,

Thank you so much for your interest! I’m happy to share a bit more about the project.

Unlike a traditional sponsored campaign, this is an AI Short Drama co-creation project. Rather than creating a single advertisement, we work together with creators to develop an original AI short drama where the creator becomes the main character.

The story is tailored around the creator’s personality, content style, and audience, allowing the final result to feel like a natural extension of their existing content rather than a traditional commercial.

How the project works

A typical AI short drama consists of 10–20 episodes.

• The first 4–5 episodes are released as free episodes to introduce the story and engage the creator’s audience.
• The remaining episodes are released on our platform as premium content, where viewers can unlock the full series.

Our goal is not simply to create a campaign, but to build a new storytelling experience together with creators.

Creative Process

The project is highly collaborative.

After we confirm the collaboration, our content team will first develop several story concepts based on the creator’s profile and content style.

The creator will have the opportunity to:

• Review the story outline
• Share ideas about the storyline, genre, or characters
• Review the final script before production begins

If there are any creative ideas you’d like to explore, we’d love to develop them together. After all, no one understands your audience better than you.

Collaboration Models

We currently offer two collaboration options:

1. Fixed Project Fee

A one-time payment for the entire AI short drama project.

2. Revenue Share

Creators receive 80% of the Net Revenue generated by the project.

For creators with highly engaged audiences, we often recommend the Revenue Share model, as it offers much stronger long-term earning potential than a traditional one-time campaign fee.

Why We’re Reaching Out

After reviewing your content, we genuinely feel your style would translate very naturally into story-driven content.

Your personality and on-camera presence would make you a great fit as the lead character of an AI short drama, and we’d love to create something unique together.
