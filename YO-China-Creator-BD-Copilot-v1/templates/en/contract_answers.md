## Net Revenue
Net Revenue refers to the revenue generated by the project after deducting reasonable and necessary costs directly related to its operation and distribution, such as payment processing fees, taxes, refunds if applicable, and other operational costs.

To ensure transparency, we will register an account for you on our platform, where you can access your dashboard and view project performance, subscription orders, and revenue data.

In addition, within two months after receiving each revenue statement, you may submit a written request for a more detailed revenue report.

## Non-compete clarification
The agreement does not prohibit you from participating in other AI-related projects or collaborations in general.

The non-compete clause is intended only to protect the specific AI character, persona, and project that we create together.
