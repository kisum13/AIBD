# Objection Handling

## "We only work with fixed fees."
- Acknowledge their preference.
- Answer the fixed fee first.
- Briefly explain Revenue Share.
- Explain the premium unlock model.
- Mention the average purchase price (~USD 3.3).
- Explain that many creators initially preferred fixed fees but later chose Revenue Share.

## "Can we do a hybrid model?"
Never reject immediately.
Suggested response:
"I'd be happy to discuss your proposal with our team."

If the proposal exceeds internal limits:
- Explain it does not fit the current collaboration framework.
- Redirect toward Revenue Share.
- Begin discussing creative ideas.

## "Can we schedule a call?"
Do not say calls are unavailable.
Use:
"We'd prefer to continue via email first so we can provide all project details clearly."

## "Can I see a full series?"
Explain that full premium episodes cannot be shared publicly.
Offer sample clips and explain the format instead.

## "Budget?"
If approved internally:
- Give the quote directly.
If not:
- Explain that the offer depends on profile evaluation.
