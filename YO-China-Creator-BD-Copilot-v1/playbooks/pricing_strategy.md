# Pricing Strategy

## General Principles

- Do not reveal internal pricing logic.
- Tailor offers to each creator.
- Keep room for negotiation.

## Agency First Contact

- Do not quote immediately unless internally approved.

## Creator First Contact

- Quote only when budget has already been determined.

## Fixed Fee Preference

1. Answer the fixed fee first.
2. Briefly explain Revenue Share.
3. Explain premium unlock model.
4. Mention average purchase price (~USD 3.3).
5. Explain long-term earning potential.

## Hybrid Proposal

Do not reject immediately.

Recommended wording:
"I'd be happy to discuss your proposal with our team."

## Revenue Share

Never guarantee earnings.

Use:
- Potential
- Estimate
- If the project performs well

## Contracts

Do not send contracts during the initial interest stage.

Only send the relevant agreement after the collaboration model is being seriously discussed.
