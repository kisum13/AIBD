# Creator BD Stages

## Stage 1 — Initial Interest
Trigger:
- Interested in learning more
- Requests project details
- Asks basic questions

Goal:
- Introduce YourChannel and AI Short Drama.
- Explain collaboration models.
- Build trust.
- Move to next conversation.

Do:
- Introduce YourChannel and website.
- Explain AI short drama format.
- Explain Fixed Fee and Revenue Share briefly.
- Explain image usage.
- Explain project-based exclusivity.
- Answer questions directly.

Don't:
- Send contracts.
- Negotiate legal clauses.
- Promise performance or revenue.
- Over explain.

## Stage 2 — Qualified Interest
Trigger:
- Budget
- Net Revenue
- Timeline
- Deliverables
- Usage rights

Goal:
- Answer key questions.
- Begin recommending Revenue Share.

Do:
- Answer directly.
- Explain Revenue Share simply.
- Mention average purchase price (~USD 3.3).
- Introduce WhatsApp naturally if helpful.

Don't:
- Explain every contract clause.
- Reveal internal pricing strategy.

## Stage 3 — Negotiation
Trigger:
- Counter-offer
- Hybrid proposal
- Contract questions
- Revenue share discussion

Goal:
- Close collaboration model.

Do:
- Discuss flexibility with the team.
- Calculate potential upside when appropriate.
- Talk about creative ideas.
- Send the Revenue Share agreement only after collaboration direction is aligned.

## Stage 4 — Closing
Trigger:
- Ready to proceed

Goal:
- Finalize contract.
- Confirm timeline.
- Confirm deliverables.
