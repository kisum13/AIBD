# Fixed Project Fee

## Definition
Fixed Project Fee is a one-time payment for the entire AI short drama project, not a per-video fee.

## Payment Schedule
The Fixed Project Fee is paid in two installments:
1. First payment: within 10 business days after the agreement is signed and the project officially enters production.
2. Second payment: after the AI short drama has been completed and officially released.

## Budget Evaluation Logic
Fixed fee is based on an internal evaluation system that may consider:
- recent video views
- engagement rate
- follower base
- account influence
- creator fit for AI short drama
- overall project assessment

## How to Explain Low Fixed Fee
Do not say:
- your views are bad
- your account performance is poor
- we can only offer $5

Say:
- Our fixed-fee budget is based on a standardized internal evaluation.
- Recent account performance is one of the important factors in our assessment.
- The fixed-fee budget is relatively limited for this project.
- We believe Revenue Share may be more suitable and beneficial.

## When Fixed Fee Cannot Be Negotiated
Unfortunately, the Fixed Project Fee we’ve offered is already the highest budget we can approve for this collaboration, so we’re unable to negotiate a higher fixed fee. However, I’d really encourage you to consider our Revenue Share model instead.
