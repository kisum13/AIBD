# Contract Terms

## Image / Likeness Usage
Creator’s image and likeness will only be used for this specific AI short drama project and its related promotion and distribution.

It does not mean:
- using creator’s image for unrelated projects
- using creator’s image for unrelated commercial campaigns
- using creator’s image for AI model training

## Worldwide License
“Worldwide” refers to the distribution and promotion scope of this specific project, because the platform distributes globally.

## Contract Term
Default: two years. In some negotiated cases: one year.

## Exclusivity / Non-compete
This is not an exclusive partnership covering all AI-related collaborations.

The clause is project-specific and protects:
- the specific AI character
- the persona
- the storyline
- highly similar roles or directly competing projects

It does not prevent creators from:
- working with other companies
- doing other AI-related projects in general
- participating in unrelated campaigns

## Net Revenue
Net Revenue means revenue generated by the project after deducting reasonable and necessary costs directly related to operation and distribution.

Possible deductions:
- third-party platform service fees
- payment processing fees
- taxes or mandatory governmental charges
- refunds or chargebacks
- other reasonable operational expenses directly related to distribution and monetization

## Transparency
- We will register an account for the creator on our platform.
- Creator can access a dashboard to view project performance, subscription orders, and revenue data.
- Within two months after receiving each revenue statement, creator may submit a written request for a more detailed revenue report.

## Minimum Guarantee
Revenue Share agreement does not include minimum guarantee or fixed payment. If creator wants guaranteed income, use Fixed Project Fee agreement.
