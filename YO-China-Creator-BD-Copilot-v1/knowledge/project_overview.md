# Project Overview

## English Core Pitch
This is an AI Short Drama project where we co-create an original AI-generated mini-series with the creator as the main character. Rather than a traditional sponsored post, the series is built around the creator’s personal brand, content style, and audience.

## 中文核心定义
这是一个 AI 短剧项目，不是传统广告。我们会围绕创作者本人形象、账号风格、受众特点，打造一部以其为主角的原创 AI 短剧。

## Standard Project Structure
- A complete AI short drama usually consists of 10–20 episodes.
- Approximately the first one-third is released for free to attract viewers.
- The remaining episodes are unlocked on our platform through a one-time purchase.
- Average purchase price is around USD 3.3 per full series.
- Production timeline is usually 3–6 weeks after signing and creative direction confirmation.
- Creators usually publish around 5 promotional posts during launch.

## What Creators Usually Post
- Free episodes
- Trailers
- Highlight clips
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Other mutually agreed promotional materials

These are not five separate brand campaigns. They are promotional materials centered around the same AI short drama.

## What Makes This Different From Ads
- Creator is the main character.
- Story is customized around creator’s style.
- Platform also distributes and promotes globally.
- Revenue Share allows creator to keep earning when project generates revenue.
