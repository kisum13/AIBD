# Content Workflow

## After Signing
1. Receive signed agreement.
2. Review and countersign.
3. Ask creator for initial ideas if any.
4. BD organizes creator’s ideas and passes to content team.
5. Content team develops outline.
6. Create group chat with creator + content team.
7. Review outline together.
8. Script development.
9. Production.
10. Release and promotion.

## What Creator Should Provide
Creator can provide:
- story direction
- character idea
- world setting
- tone and atmosphere
- emotional theme
- scenes they want to explore

Creator does not need to provide:
- full script
- every scene
- detailed dialogue
- shot-by-shot design

If creator gives too much detail: thank them, say we understand the overall direction, and content team will develop the outline professionally.
