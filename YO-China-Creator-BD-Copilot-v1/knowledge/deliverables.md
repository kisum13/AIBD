# Deliverables

Creators usually publish around 5 promotional posts during the launch period.

These may include:
- free episodes
- trailers
- highlight clips
- behind-the-scenes
- other mutually agreed promotional materials

The 5 posts are not five separate brand campaigns. They are all related to the same AI short drama project.

The full series is released on the platform. Creator social posts introduce the story and guide viewers to unlock the full series.

Bio link timing and duration should be coordinated according to the project launch schedule. Avoid promising a fixed duration unless confirmed by contract.
