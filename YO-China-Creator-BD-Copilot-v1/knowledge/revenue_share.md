# Revenue Share

## Standard Model
Creators receive 80% of the Net Revenue generated by the project.

## Why We Recommend It
- Most recent creator partners have chosen Revenue Share.
- It offers stronger long-term earning potential than a one-time fixed fee.
- Platform and creator are aligned because both benefit from better performance.
- Strong story + creator audience + platform distribution can create better upside.

## User Payment Logic
- A complete AI short drama is unlocked through a one-time purchase.
- Average purchase price: around USD 3.3 per series.

## Important Disclaimer
Never guarantee exact earnings. Use:
- potential
- estimate
- if the project performs well
- actual results depend on story, audience engagement, promotion, and other factors

## Strong Pitch
Under this model, instead of receiving only a one-time payment, creators continue earning as long as the series generates revenue. For creators with strong audiences, this may offer significantly higher upside.
