# Fixed Fee Reply Examples

## 1. Creator asks: Is the fixed fee per video?

Hi,

Just to clarify, the Fixed Project Fee is for the entire AI short drama project, not a payment per individual video or post.

The project usually includes the AI short drama itself and around five promotional contents during the launch period, such as free episodes, trailers, highlights, or other mutually agreed promotional materials.

---

## 2. Creator asks: When will I receive the fixed fee?

Hi,

For the Fixed Project Fee model, payment is made in two installments.

The first payment is made within 10 business days after the agreement is signed, the script is confirmed, the necessary materials are provided, and the project officially enters production.

The second payment is made within 10 business days after the AI short drama is officially released online and the release conditions are verified.

---

## 3. Creator asks: Can I get paid upfront?

Hi,

Our standard Fixed Project Fee model follows a two-installment payment schedule.

The first payment is made after the agreement is signed, the script is confirmed, the required materials are provided, and production officially begins. The second payment is made after the project is officially released online and verified.

So under the standard agreement, we are not able to provide full upfront payment before production begins.

---

## 4. Creator asks: Can you increase the fixed fee?

Hi,

Thank you for your question. The Fixed Project Fee is based on our internal evaluation and the current project budget, including factors such as recent account performance, audience engagement, and overall project fit.

At the moment, the fixed-fee budget we shared is the highest we can approve for this collaboration.

That said, I would honestly recommend considering our Revenue Share model instead, especially if you are interested in stronger long-term earning potential.

---

## 5. Creator sends a high rate card

Hi,

Thank you so much for sharing your rate card. We completely understand your standard pricing and the value of your audience.

This collaboration is structured differently from a traditional sponsored post. It is a project-based AI short drama, where our team handles story development, AI production, editing, localization, platform release, and global distribution.

The Fixed Project Fee we can offer is based on our internal evaluation for the full project. If the fixed-fee budget does not align with your standard rate, I would strongly recommend considering the Revenue Share model instead, as it may offer stronger long-term upside if the project performs well.

---

## 6. Creator asks: How many posts do I need to publish?

Hi,

Creators usually publish around five promotional contents during the launch period. These may include free episodes, trailers, highlight clips, behind-the-scenes materials, or other mutually agreed promotional content.

These five posts are all part of the same AI short drama project and are not treated as separate brand campaigns.

---

## 7. Creator asks: Do I get extra payment for the five posts?

Hi,

Under the Fixed Project Fee model, the agreed project fee already covers the promotional obligations for the project, including the agreed promotional posts.

So there is no additional payment based on the number, format, or timing of the promotional content, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

---

## 8. Creator asks: Can we do fixed fee + revenue share?

Hi,

I completely understand why a hybrid model would be attractive.

At the moment, our standard collaboration structure supports either a Fixed Project Fee or Revenue Share, rather than a combination of both.

If you prefer guaranteed compensation, the Fixed Project Fee model may be suitable. If you are more interested in long-term upside, I would recommend Revenue Share.

---

## 9. Creator asks: Will my image be used for AI training?

Hi,

No. Your likeness authorization is limited to this specific AI short drama project and its related promotion/distribution.

It does not authorize general-purpose AI model training or unrelated commercial use.

---

## 10. Creator asks: Can I work with other AI companies?

Hi,

Yes, the agreement is not intended to prevent you from working with other AI companies in general.

The restriction is project-specific and mainly protects the AI character, role setting, and project concept created through this collaboration, especially against highly similar or directly competing works.

---

## 11. Creator asks: Can the contract be one year?

Hi,

The attached agreement reflects our current standard terms.

If we move toward the final stage of the collaboration and the contract term becomes a key concern, I’d be happy to discuss internally whether there is any flexibility.

For now, I’d recommend reviewing the standard agreement first and letting me know any specific concerns you may have.

---

## 12. Creator asks: Can I review the final video before release?

Hi,

Yes. You will be able to preview the completed work before final release and provide reasonable comments if you believe the use of your authorized image does not comply with the agreement or may negatively affect your public image.

---

## 13. Creator asks: Who owns the AI drama?

Hi,

The platform owns the intellectual property rights in the final AI short drama and related project materials, including the script, AI-generated assets, and final work.

Your personal likeness rights remain yours, but the project works themselves are owned and operated by the platform.

---

## 14. Creator asks: How long does the tracking link need to stay?

Hi,

The official tracking link should remain accessible for at least 60 consecutive days after the first public release of the project.

We’ll coordinate with you before launch on where to place the link, such as your profile, homepage, pinned post, or pinned comment.

---

## 15. Creator asks: Which model do you recommend?

Hi,

It depends on your preference.

If you prefer guaranteed compensation, the Fixed Project Fee model may be more suitable.

If you are open to long-term upside and want to participate in the project’s future performance, I would honestly recommend Revenue Share, especially for creators with strong audience engagement.
