# Fixed Fee Agreement Summary

> Internal AI-readable summary. Use this for replying to creator questions about the Fixed Project Fee agreement. Do not send this file directly to creators.

## 1. Cooperation Model

The Fixed Fee Agreement is used when the creator chooses a one-time fixed project fee instead of Revenue Share.

Under this model:

- Party A / YourChannel uses Party B’s authorized likeness, image, voice, and related personality rights to produce at least one AI-generated film or television work.
- Each work shall consist of no fewer than ten episodes.
- Party A is responsible for project creation, production, post-production, distribution, and operation.
- Party B receives the agreed fixed cooperation fee according to the payment milestones.

Important external wording:

> This is a project-based collaboration, not a payment per individual post or per video.

## 2. Standard Project Explanation

A complete AI short drama project usually includes:

- AI-generated short drama series
- Approximately 10–20 episodes
- Free promotional episodes or clips
- Full series released on platform
- Around five promotional contents during launch

When creators compare this with normal brand posts, explain:

> This is different from a traditional sponsored post. The creator becomes the main character in an original AI short drama, and the promotional posts are all connected to the same project.

## 3. Story and Script Workflow

The platform may provide multiple story outline options.

The creator may select one story direction.

After selection:

- Party A independently develops the script, characters, dialogue, plot, and ending.
- Party A retains final creative decision-making authority.
- The creator may review the script for image protection concerns.
- The creator may preview the completed final work before release.

## 4. Image Protection

The project content should not intentionally distort, defame, slander, or materially harm the creator’s public image, reputation, or commercial goodwill.

The content should not include illegal, pornographic, violent, discriminatory, or grossly inappropriate material.

External wording:

> You will be able to review the project content for image protection concerns, and we will make sure the project does not intentionally harm your public image or reputation.

## 5. Authorization Scope

The creator authorizes project-specific use of:

- Electronic likeness
- Facial features
- Body characteristics
- Voice characteristics
- Related personality rights

Authorization is limited to the mutually confirmed AI-generated project.

It does not constitute authorization for general-purpose AI model training.

## 6. What Authorization Does NOT Include

Party A shall not use the creator’s likeness for the following without separate agreement:

- Independent advertising endorsements
- Livestream e-commerce
- Brand collaborations
- Other unrelated commercial activities

External wording:

> Your likeness will only be used for this specific AI short drama project and its related promotion/distribution. Any unrelated advertising, brand collaboration, or livestream commerce use would require a separate agreement.

## 7. Agreement Term

The standard fixed-fee agreement term is two years in the current contract version.

Internal guardrail:

- Do not proactively offer one-year term.
- If creator asks for shorter term and is close to signing, say you can discuss internally.
- Do not promise a revised term unless user explicitly approves.

## 8. Creator Promotion Obligations

The creator is expected to publish promotional content for the project according to the promotional schedule.

The fixed fee covers all promotional obligations under the agreement.

## 9. Five Promotional Contents

Creator shall produce and publish no fewer than five promotional contents for the project.

These may include:

- Full works
- Highlights
- Trailers
- Behind-the-scenes footage
- Other agreed promotional materials

Important explanation:

- These five contents are part of the same project.
- They are not five separate commercial collaborations.
- They do not require additional fees.
- The fixed project fee covers the project promotion obligations.

External wording:

> The five promotional posts are all related to the same AI short drama project and do not count as five separate brand campaigns.

## 10. Official Tracking Link

The creator should display the unique official tracking link provided by Party A within three business days after first public release.

The link should remain accessible for at least 60 consecutive days from first public release.

After the 60-day placement obligation is fulfilled, the creator has no further obligation to maintain the link unless otherwise agreed in writing.

## 11. Fixed Fee Payment

The fixed cooperation fee is paid in two installments.

Current contract example:

- Total cooperation fee: USD 3,300
- First payment: USD 1,650
- Second payment: USD 1,650

Important: the amount is a contract variable and should be updated based on the creator’s actual negotiated fee. Do not use USD 3,300 in external emails unless it is the approved offer for that creator.

## 12. First Payment Condition

The first payment is made within 10 business days after all of the following conditions are met:

- Agreement signed
- Script mutually confirmed
- Creator photo materials, voice characteristics, and other required materials confirmed and provided
- Production phase begins

External simplified wording:

> The first payment is made within 10 business days after the agreement is signed, the necessary materials are provided, the script is confirmed, and the project officially enters production.

## 13. Second Payment Condition

The second payment is made within 10 business days after official online release of the cooperative AI work.

The release date is the date when:

- Finalized episodes are released
- Official tracking link is placed
- Party A verifies release conditions

External simplified wording:

> The second payment is made within 10 business days after the project is officially released online and verified.

## 14. No Additional Fees After Payment

After both fixed-fee installments are completed, Party A is considered to have fulfilled all payment obligations for this cooperation.

The creator should not claim additional rights or payments from the same project cooperation.

## 15. Settlement Currency

Payment is made in USD.

If payment is made in another currency, exchange rate on the payment date applies, and cross-border processing fees may be included in project costs.

## 16. Sales Data Verification

Even under the fixed-fee agreement, the creator may request Party A to verify sales data and Net Revenue once every two months.

This is a transparency provision, not an additional revenue entitlement unless separately agreed.

## 17. Intellectual Property

All project results and works belong exclusively to Party A after creation.

This includes:

- Completed AI-generated films and television dramas
- Raw footage
- Character settings
- Scripts
- Dialogue
- Storyboards
- Visual designs
- Digital human image data
- AI-generated intermediate products
- Final works

The creator retains personality rights but does not own copyright property rights in the project works.

## 18. Distribution and Commercial Operation

Party A has the right to use, reproduce, distribute, adapt, translate, publicly transmit, commercially operate, and authorize third parties to use the works globally.

External wording should remain simple:

> The platform owns and operates the final AI short drama project and handles distribution and commercial operation.

## 19. Exclusivity / Non-compete Explanation

The fixed-fee agreement contains restrictions around highly similar works, character positioning, same theme, similar genre, and overlapping target audience.

Internal external reply guardrail:

Do not say:

- “You cannot do any AI work.”
- “You are exclusive to us.”
- “You cannot work with other companies.”

Say:

> The restriction is project-specific and mainly protects the AI character, role setting, and project concept created through this collaboration. It does not prohibit unrelated AI or commercial collaborations in general.

## 20. Confidentiality

The creator should keep confidential:

- Full scripts
- Episode summaries
- Character settings
- Unpublished materials
- Production budgets
- Cost details
- Marketing strategies
- ROI data
- Contract content
- Compensation terms
- Financial information

## 21. Internal Reply Guardrails

DO:
- Emphasize this is a project fee, not per-post fee.
- Explain two payment milestones clearly.
- Clarify that the five posts are part of one project.
- Avoid saying fixed fee is negotiable unless user approves.
- Recommend Revenue Share if creator requests higher fixed fee and fixed budget is limited.

DO NOT:
- Promise upfront payment before production.
- Promise 100% payment upon signing.
- Promise higher fixed fee without approval.
- Promise shorter term without approval.
- Describe exclusivity as a blanket AI restriction.
