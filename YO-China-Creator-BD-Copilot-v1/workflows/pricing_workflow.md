# Pricing Workflow

## Creator asks if price is negotiable
- Thank them.
- Fixed fee is highest approved / limited.
- Recommend Revenue Share.
- Offer to send Revenue Share agreement.

## Creator sends high rate card
- Appreciate rate card.
- Explain this is AI short drama, not normal sponsored post.
- State max fixed project fee if user provides.
- Explain deliverables around 5 posts.
- Recommend Revenue Share.

## Creator asks hybrid
- Understand appeal.
- Current standardized structure only supports Fixed Fee or Revenue Share.
- Recommend Revenue Share.
- If manager has many creators, invite future creator recommendations after first collaboration.

## Fixed fee very low
- Do not mention exact tiny amount unless instructed.
- Say fixed fee cannot reach several hundred dollars.
- Compliment visual / style fit if true.
- Recommend Revenue Share.
