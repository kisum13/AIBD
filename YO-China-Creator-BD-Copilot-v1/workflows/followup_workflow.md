# Follow-up Workflow

## After Proposal
Warm, light. Ask if they had chance to review.

## After Contract
Project moving actively but no pressure. Mention production resources can be arranged earlier if confirmed soon.

## Network Issue
Sorry, I’m not sure if it was due to a network issue on our side, but we didn’t receive your messages properly. Could you please resend them?

## WhatsApp Issue
For now, email may be the safest way to avoid missing any important information.
