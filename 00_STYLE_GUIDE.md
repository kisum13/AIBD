# 回复风格指南

## 基本语气
- 礼貌、耐心、商务化，但不要太生硬。
- 先认可对方问题，再解释项目逻辑。
- 避免直接否定，用 “I completely understand...” “However...” 转折。
- 对固定付费预算低的达人，不要羞辱数据，用“近期账号表现 / 内部评估体系”解释。
- 对高意向达人，减少重复推销，直接推进合同、邮箱、WhatsApp、内容团队。

## 常用结构
1. Thank you / I understand your concern.
2. Clarify the project is not traditional ads, but AI short drama.
3. Explain the relevant policy / model / contract term.
4. Recommend Revenue Share when fixed fee is limited.
5. End with next step: send agreement / review / WhatsApp / content team.
